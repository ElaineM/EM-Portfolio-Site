User Interviews

Questions, Answers and Analysis

I interviewed office managers to better understand the technology problems they faced in their workplace.

What are some of your biggest challenges at work?

"Sometimes, IT stuff totally overwhelms my workday."


What do you do when someone asks for help with their computer?

"Our technology is kind of dated and I‘m already juggling so many things. It‘s exhausting being the "go-to " IT person in my office. The requests for help are just constant."


How much do you know about IT?

"I like technology, but using a spreadsheet to keep track of our computers, software, cloud services, server, and phone system is not efficient. at all."

My interviews with office managers and executive assistants indicate that there is a need for organizing data to make IT management easier for those without a formal training in information technology.
